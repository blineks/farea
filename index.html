🍔 Hamburger

Emoji Meaning

A burger with a beef patty, usually depicted on a sesame bun with cheese, lettuce, and tomato—making it a cheeseburger, though officially called Hamburger by Unicode.

Platforms stack the ingredients in various orders. Google previously placed the cheese under the patty, a "controversy" CEO Sundar Pichai addressed in 2018. 

Hamburger was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
